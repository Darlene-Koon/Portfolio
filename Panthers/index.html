HISTORY

  • The Carolina Panthers defeat the Jacksonville Jaguars 20-14 in the Hall of Fame Game in Canton, OH. The Panthers become the first team in NFL history to win its inaugural contest.
  • The Panthers lose 23-20 in overtime to the Atlanta Falcons in the team's first regular-season game.
  • The Panthers win their first game in franchise history, defeating the New York Jets 26-15.
  • Construction begins on the most significant renovations to Bank of America Stadium since opening in 1996.
